# Olive

Olive is a personal carbon tracker built around the things you already do. It reads your travel patterns and purchase receipts quietly in the background, without questionnaires or manual logging, and gives you a real number built from your real life.

Most people want to live more sustainably but have no idea where their carbon actually comes from. Olive tracks it. You see exactly when your choices start to matter.

Coming soon on iOS and Android. Part of the SIGNAL research initiative.
